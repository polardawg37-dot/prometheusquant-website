Who We Are

A partnership built around markets.

Prometheus Strategies is led by four partners with a shared interest in markets and a common approach to trading them.

Our Story

Sean Thomas, Shane McDermott, Bryce Lesinski, and Andrew Wu met at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business and founded Prometheus Strategies to trade prediction markets, a new class of exchange-listed contracts that was growing quickly and attracting serious capital.

The firm was built around that opportunity: systematic research, technology developed in-house, and a deliberate approach to risk.
