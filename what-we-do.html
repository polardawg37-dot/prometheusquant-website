What We Do

We trade prediction markets.

Exchange-listed contracts on the outcomes of real-world events, in sports and beyond.

The Markets

CFTC-regulated exchanges now list contracts on thousands of real-world outcomes, from the result of a game to the path of the economy. It is a broad and rapidly maturing market, and it rewards the same discipline as any established asset class.

Prometheus develops systematic approaches and applies them across markets, adapting what works from one to the next.

How We Work

The principles behind every position.

Research driven

Every view begins with data. We build models, test them against outcomes, and revise as the evidence changes.

Priced in probabilities

These markets trade in probabilities, and reasoning under uncertainty is the core of the job.

Deliberately selective

Not every market is worth trading. We choose positions carefully and pass on the rest.

Built to improve

Results feed back into research. Every settled market makes the next model better.

Technology

We build our own systems.

Research, pricing, and execution at Prometheus run on technology designed and developed in-house. Owning the full stack gives us control over how we trade and lets the firm adapt as markets evolve.
