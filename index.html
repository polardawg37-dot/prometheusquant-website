About Us

Built on Signal,
Not Speculation

Prometheus Quant is a quantitative research and trading firm built on the Prometheus model — a fully automated equity trading system that identifies and exploits short-term mean-reversion in liquid US equities.

At its core, Prometheus scans a carefully curated universe of 285 names across 11 GICS sectors, detecting statistically abnormal price dislocations relative to SPY-beta-adjusted expected returns. Every trade is the product of systematic conviction scoring, not intuition.

Risk is not an afterthought. ATR-calibrated stop losses, quarter-Kelly position sizing, sector exposure limits, and correlation-cluster caps work in concert to preserve the edge under realistic trading conditions — through regime shifts, volatility spikes, and the full complexity of live markets.
