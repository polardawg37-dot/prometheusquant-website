About

A Firm Built on
Compounding Research

Prometheus Quant is a systematic trading firm operating through a proprietary research platform, built from the ground up to identify genuine statistical edges, stress-test them against reality, and deploy only what survives.

The platform's core principle is that each experiment, live trade, and market regime encountered becomes data for the next generation of research. Over time, the system does not just trade: it learns which hypotheses to pursue and which to discard before capital is at risk.

Every strategy that reaches live markets has earned its place through rigorous out-of-sample validation. No shortcuts. No intuition substituting for evidence. The edge compounds because the research infrastructure does.

Approach

The Research
Methodology

Prometheus operates on a set of non-negotiable principles, enforced by the system as architectural constraints, not left to researcher discretion.

01
Statistical Integrity

Every backtest carries significance tests. Multiple-testing bias is corrected at the factor screening stage. Walk-forward validation is a hard gate: a strategy that cannot survive it does not proceed.

02
Economic Causality

A signal must have a plausible mechanism. Spurious correlations that fit the data but cannot be explained do not earn deployment. The model must make sense before it is trusted with capital.

03
Execution Realism

Math that works on paper but fails under realistic cost assumptions is not an edge. Every strategy is stress-tested against conservative slippage, transaction costs, and adverse fill scenarios.

04
Honest Attribution

Every live dollar of P&L is attributed. Every fill is benchmarked. Survivorship bias, data quality gaps, and model assumptions are documented, not hidden, because accountability is the foundation of improvement.

05
Layered Risk Controls

Risk management is architectural, not reactive. Position limits, correlation constraints, drawdown gates, and regime detection operate in concert, bounding worst-case outcomes before they materialize.

06
Publish-Ready Standards

Research outputs are produced to institutional quality: tearsheets, regime breakdowns, signal scorecards. If the work cannot be presented clearly and honestly, it is not complete.
